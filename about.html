Why Voluntrix

Voluntrix was created with a simple vision — to make helping others easy and accessible. Many people are willing to donate or volunteer but often do not know where to begin. Our platform bridges that gap by connecting donors, volunteers, and NGOs.

Foundation of Voluntrix

Voluntrix was founded at the prestigious Megha Institute of Engineering and Technology. The institute provided its best resources to support the creation of this revolutionary idea. The management offered strong support through staff guidance, helping us throughout the journey of building our mission — Voluntrix.
